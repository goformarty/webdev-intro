Hedghogs:

Hedgehogs are famous for their prickly spines, which they have everywhere except on their face, legs and bellies.


These cute critters depend on their spines for defense,by curling into a tight ball and tucking in their heads, tail and legs, they protect vulnerable parts of their body.

Cute hedgehog facts:

  1. The name for a baby hedgehog is a hoglet.
  2. They are quite good swimmers and can climb trees, too!
  3. When they are born their spines are soft and short.
